Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Porch Light Software ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FastTrack, MoodTrack, and SubTrack iOS applications. This policy explains how we handle information in our apps.

Data We Do Not Collect

All app data (fasting logs, mood entries, subscription records) is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of this data. There are no user accounts, no cloud sync, and no data leaves your device.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our free apps display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA), if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Approximate location (country/region)
  • Device type and OS version
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

You will be asked for permission before any tracking occurs. You can change your preference at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information on how Google uses this data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Remove Ads

Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will no longer see ads. No tracking data will be collected by AdMob after this purchase.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at support@porchlightsoftware.com.
