Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to a server or the cloud. Your fasting history, settings, and protocols never leave your phone.

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Remove Ads". This is a one-time purchase of $4.99 — no subscription.

I purchased Remove Ads but still see ads

Try restarting the app. If ads persist, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". If the issue continues, contact us below.

Can I transfer my data to a new phone?

Your data transfers automatically when you restore from a device backup (Google backup or iCloud backup). We do not currently support manual data export.

Is fasting safe?

FastTrack is a timer and tracking tool, not a medical device. We cannot provide medical advice. Please consult a healthcare professional before starting any fasting regimen.
