Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Porch Light Software ("we", "our", or "us") built FastTrack as a free, ad-supported app with an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove ads. This page explains how we handle your information.

The Short Version

Your fasting data never leaves your device. We don't have accounts, servers, or cloud storage. Everything you log in FastTrack stays on your phone.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Fasting sessions, timers, and history
  • Custom protocols and settings
  • Notes and personal entries
  • Health or medical information
  • Names, email addresses, or account information

All of the above is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Advertising

FastTrack uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Device information (model, OS version)

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. You can also remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Analytics

FastTrack does not include any analytics SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

FastTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@porchlightsoftware.com.
