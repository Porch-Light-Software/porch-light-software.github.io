Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Porch Light Software ("we", "our", or "us") built TileDrop as a free, ad-supported app. This page explains how we handle your information.

The Short Version

Your game data never leaves your device. We don't have accounts, servers, or cloud storage. Everything you play in TileDrop stays on your phone.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Game progress, scores, and statistics
  • Game history and high scores
  • Settings and preferences
  • Names, email addresses, or account information

All of the above is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Advertising

TileDrop uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Usage data and diagnostics

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

TileDrop does not include any analytics SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

TileDrop is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@porchlightsoftware.com.
