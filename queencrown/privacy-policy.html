Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Porch Light Software ("we", "our", or "us") built QueenCrown as a free, ad-supported app. This page explains how we handle your information.

The Short Version

Your game data never leaves your device. We don't have accounts, servers, or cloud storage. Everything you play in QueenCrown stays on your phone.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Puzzle progress, solve times, and statistics
  • Game history and streaks
  • Settings and preferences
  • Names, email addresses, or account information

All of the above is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Advertising

QueenCrown uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads and optional rewarded video ads (used to earn hints). AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Usage data and diagnostics

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, QueenCrown uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. The first time you open the app, you will be asked whether to allow tracking. If you decline, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads and will not access the IDFA for cross-app tracking. Your choice is respected and you can change it at any time in iOS Settings.

In-App Purchases

QueenCrown offers a one-time "Remove Ads" in-app purchase. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see or store your payment details. Apple provides us with an anonymous receipt used only to validate your purchase on your device. For details on how Apple handles purchase data, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

QueenCrown does not include any analytics SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

QueenCrown is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@porchlightsoftware.com.
